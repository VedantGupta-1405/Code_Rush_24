FAQ

Common questions.

CODERUSHis open to all college students - undergraduate and postgraduate - from any institution. You do not need to be a CMRIT student to participate. Simply form a team of 2-4 members and register.

Teams can have between 2 and 4 members. Solo participation is not allowed.

Bring your laptop, charger, college ID, and any hardware you plan to use. We will provide power strips, Wi-Fi, food, and a great atmosphere. Sleeping mats will be available for overnight stays.

Projects are judged on innovation & creativity, technical complexity, practical impact, presentation quality, and completeness. Judges include industry professionals and faculty from CMRIT.

All code written during the hackathon must be original. You may use publicly available libraries, frameworks, and APIs. Using a pre-existing codebase as the core of your project is not permitted.
