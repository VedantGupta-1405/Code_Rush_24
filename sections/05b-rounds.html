Rounds

Round Description

Round 1

Idea to Direction

Teams present their problem statement, core idea, and proposed solution flow. This round focuses on clarity of thought, feasibility, innovation, and how well the team can explain the value of what they plan to build.

Round 2

Build to Demo

Shortlisted teams showcase their working solution, product experience, and final pitch. Evaluation centers on execution, technical depth, real-world relevance, presentation, and the impact of the final prototype.
